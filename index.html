About the company

Independent by design

Founded in 2019, Henry & Tosh Limited is a private investment company registered in England & Wales under Company No. 11919030.

We focus on disciplined execution of synthetic strategies designed to extract income and directional alpha from high-beta equities. The company operates exclusively with its own capital and does not accept or manage client funds.

Investment strategy

A repeatable operating rhythm

Synthetic long + covered call

Our core model establishes synthetic long exposure through options combinations, then writes calls against that exposure to pursue both directional participation and option premium income.

Risk management framework

Defined position parameters, liquidity reserves and drawdown limits are applied before entry. Exposure is actively monitored against market conditions and volatility.

Weekly execution cycle

The portfolio is reviewed and recalibrated every Monday morning, with positions actively managed throughout the week to optimise risk and reward.
